Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-11

1. Introduction

Thank you for using ALFI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data regarding your usage of our natural cycle tracking utility.

2. Data We Collect

To calculate precise astronomical positions and personal biorhythm models, we may collect the following information:

  • Calculation Data: Date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth.

This data is strictly required to generate deterministic astronomical coordinates (ephemerides) necessary for the MSA Framework algorithms.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for mathematical calculations, cycle modeling, and generating analytical insights within the app. Specifically:

  • We use this data to calculate planetary positions and visualize interaction dynamics.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • All calculations are performed securely according to platform requirements.

4. In-App Purchases

Payment information for subscriptions (ALFI Premium) is processed exclusively by the App Stores. We do not have access to your payment details or credit card information.

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may delete your account and all associated information at any time using the option provided in the app (Settings > Delete Account). Upon deletion, your personal profile is permanently removed.

6. Children's Privacy

ALFI is not intended for children under the age specified in local laws. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at: astrodiary.dar@gmail.com
