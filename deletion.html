Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can delete your ALFI account and all associated data at any time using one of the methods described below.

Option 1: Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the ALFI app:

  1. Open the app and navigate to Hub
  2. Tap on Profile
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted

Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed immediately.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you can request deletion by emailing us. Please include the email address associated with your ALFI account.

astrodiary.dar@gmail.com

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 48 hours.

What Data Is Deleted?

Upon account deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials
  • All personal birth data and saved contacts
  • Bond analyses and compatibility records
  • Subscription and payment history references
  • All app preferences and settings

This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
